About Our Project

Over the years FPS games become popular, the real question is do we really benefit from this games? Our project aims to explore the potential benefits of playing First Person Shooter (FPS) video games on our reaction time and hand-eye coordination. We aim to provide insights into whether FPS games can indeed enhance these cognitive abilities.
